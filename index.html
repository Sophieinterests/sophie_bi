About Me

As a passionate Speech Pathologist, I work in developing interventions for children with a range of speech and language disorders, aiming to improve communication outcomes. I also work in gender voice training, helping those with gender dysphoria find their voice. I am currently expanding my skill set by learning computer science at the University of New South Wales, with the goal of leveraging technology to create innovative solutions in health. I am driven by a desire to enhance the quality of life for vulnerable populations.
